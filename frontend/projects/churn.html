Machine Learning Project

Customer Churn Prediction

Predicting customer attrition to support proactive retention strategies

This project focuses on identifying customers at risk of churn using supervised machine learning models. It is designed to demonstrate end-to-end data science workflows, from feature engineering and model training to business interpretation and dashboarding.

Business Problem

Customer churn directly impacts revenue and growth. The goal of this project is to predict which customers are likely to churn so that targeted retention strategies can be applied before loss occurs.

Dataset

Historical customer data including demographics, usage behaviour, contract details, and service interactions. Features are engineered to capture engagement, tenure, and risk indicators.

Models (Planned)

Logistic Regression, Random Forest, and Gradient Boosting models will be evaluated using precision, recall, ROC-AUC, and business-aligned metrics.

Outputs

Model performance evaluation, feature importance analysis, and an executive-ready dashboard highlighting churn risk segments and recommended actions.
