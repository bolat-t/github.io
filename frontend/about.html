About Me

Data Scientist | Machine Learning Engineer | Problem Solver

Hello, I'm Bolat

I'm passionate about transforming raw data into meaningful insights that drive business decisions. With a strong foundation in statistics, machine learning, and software engineering, I bridge the gap between complex technical solutions and practical business applications.

My journey in data science began with curiosity about patterns in data and has evolved into a career focused on building predictive models, designing data pipelines, and creating interactive visualizations that tell compelling stories.

My Story

My fascination with data started during my undergraduate studies in Computer Science, where I discovered the power of algorithms to uncover hidden patterns. This led me to pursue advanced studies in Data Science, diving deep into machine learning, statistical modeling, and big data technologies.

Throughout my career, I've worked on diverse projects ranging from customer churn prediction to real-time sentiment analysis. Each project has taught me the importance of not just building accurate models, but also ensuring they are interpretable, scalable, and aligned with business objectives.

I believe the best data science work happens at the intersection of technical expertise and domain knowledge. That's why I invest time in understanding the business context, collaborating with stakeholders, and translating complex findings into actionable recommendations.

Machine Learning

Business Strategy
